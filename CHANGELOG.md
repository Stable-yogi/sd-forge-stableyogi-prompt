# Changelog

## v1.5.0
- **Fairer quota use:** prompts are now fetched exactly as needed — one per generation — so your
  hourly/daily allowance is spent 1:1 with the prompts you actually use, instead of in bulk.

## v1.4.0
- Added a **Refresh** button to clear stored prompts and pull a fresh set.

## v1.3.0
- Reliability and efficiency improvements.
- Simplified the panel (removed the prompt-seed field).

## v1.2.x
- Brand icon in the panel and next to the prompt box.
- Compact auto-fetch toggle in the prompt-box corner.
- UI polish and cleanup.

## v1.1.0
- Auto-fetch toggle and in-panel controls.

## v1.0.0
- Initial release.
